Color system — 4 codified + 1 utility accent

The palette, re-balanced.

Foundation is dark indigo, near-black behind the hero, warm off-white for type. Glow is the single interaction color — it's where the brand spark lives. Copper is an optional warm accent reserved for bar/cocktail moments and editorial divider lines (matches the BEER HELPS signage in the venue). The fuchsia / orange / yellow accents from the original palette are not in the system — they appear only on dedicated sub-pages (arcade, special events) where they're earned.

Typography — 2 typefaces (3 in production)

Type as architecture.

Proxima Nova for display and UI. Yorkten Slab for body. Both are brand-spec, both via Adobe Fonts. Sized large enough to read as architecture (Pints & Paddle / Swingers / Flight Club all do this). Display weight is Extra Condensed Black — heavy, condensed, signage-feeling. Body is regular slab — gives editorial weight without feeling stuffy. Reasoning: two typefaces holds the moody/confident thesis; a third would push us into Flowfest-collage territory.

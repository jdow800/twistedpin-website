## Color Palette

The palette is described as "a vivid celebration of the night and neon lights." The cooler blues/indigos do the heavy lifting; the bright accents (fuchsia, orange, yellow) are intentional sparks — use them like a strike, not a default.

### Core Colors (use freely)

| Name | HEX | CMYK |
|---|---|---|
| **Midnight Indigo** | `#200F53` | C20 M27 Y0 K67 |
| **Nautical Blue** | `#128198` | C53 M9 Y0 K40 |
| **Glow in the Dark** | `#4EECC4` | C62 M0 Y16 K7 |
| **Celestial Blue** | `#468CC8` | C51 M24 Y0 K22 |

### Accent Colors (use strategically — sparingly)

| Name | HEX | CMYK |
|---|---|---|
| **Flirty Fuchsia** | `#D45D92` | C0 M47 Y26 K17 |
| **Tango IPA** (Mango Tango) | `#FF8000` | C0 M50 Y100 K0 |
| **Lemon Twist** | `#FFE236` | C0 M11 Y79 K0 |

> Guideline from the source: "Be strategic with Flirty Fuchsia, Mango Tango, and Lemon Twist — like a perfect strike, they're brilliant when used just right, but overdo it, and you risk overpowering the subtler hues."

### Color Associations (suggested usage)

- **Bowling** — Celestial Blue / Nautical Blue
- **Cocktails & Beer** — Tango IPA / Flirty Fuchsia
- **Arcade** — Glow in the Dark / Lemon Twist accents

## Typography

All brand fonts are available free at **fonts.adobe.com** with a Creative Cloud subscription. For internal/non-design use without Adobe access: **Arial** and **Times New Roman** are acceptable fallbacks.

### Primary Brand Fonts

| Font | Role |
|---|---|
| **Proxima Nova** | Main typeface — 20+ styles (weights, widths, italics). Headlines, UI, general use. |
| **Yorkten Slab** | Body copy. Modern, robust, distinctive. |

### Secondary / Display Fonts (accents only — "the icing")

- **Proxima Nova Extra Condensed** — tight headlines, vertical stacks
- **Rajdhani** — geometric, technical
- **Gala** — display
- **Ohm** — display, character
- **Luxie** — display, character
- **Yorkten Slab Ext Thin** — light display variant
- **Thirsty Script Bold** — script accent for personality / "Date Night," birthdays, etc.

> The secondary fonts are flavor, not foundation. Default to Proxima Nova + Yorkten Slab; reach for the others only when a piece needs character.

## Logo System

### Versions Available
- **Stacked** and **side-by-side** (horizontal) lockups
- Available in **black, white, and each brand color**
- Tagline version with `GAME · BOWL · EAT · DRINK` included

### Logo + Custom Sub-text Rules
When pairing the wordmark with custom text (location, sub-brand, etc.):
1. **Vertical spacing:** Maintain a clear distance from the bottom of the wordmark to the top of a capital W in your text.
2. **Width constraint:** Confine your custom text to the width of the word "Paddle" in the wordmark.

If using the standard tagline, always use the provided lockup files — do not recreate.

## Visual Assets / Design System

### "The Pin"
A sleek distillation of the Twisted Pin logo — the emblem's core as a standalone icon. Use for small spaces, social avatars, or quick visual recognition where the full wordmark is too much.

### Formation (Bowling Lane Arrows)
Triangle shapes inspired by bowling lane targeting arrows. Use individually or grouped. They embody precision and serve as directional/compositional anchors in layouts.

### Favicon
16×16 px micro-mark. Web-only; never replaces the main logo.

## Dos / Don'ts

### ✅ DO

- Use brand colors only
